Attractions

Nelson Mandela Museum

Visit the musuem if you are looking for a cultural experience,insights into the life of Nelson Mandela. The musuem offers guided tours and a heritage trail that follows the late leaders footsteps. Cultural offerings, such as traditional dance and food are available by arrangement. - 36 minute drive

Nelson Mandela Youth & Heritage Centre

Located in the Qunu village, where Nelson Mandela's home is and where he spent his childhood. Exhibitions and tours offered at the museum. - 12 minute drive

Coffee Bay

Small town situated on the Wild Coast of the Eastern Cape. Activities such as cliff jumping, Xhosa cultural experience, surf lessons and coaching on offer - 1 hour drive

Hole in the wall

Provides some of the most beautiful scenery in the country. Perfect way to explore the Transkei coast. Also great for those that enjoy outdoor activities as it offers bird watching, nature walks, great hiking trails and fishing. - 1 hour 30 minute drive

Umtata Country club - Golf Course

Located in the O.R Tambo district municipality with a length of 3.1 kilometres. 18 hole course with driving range, putting greens, rental trolleys and carts, clubhouse, snack bar with practise and banquet facilities. - 22 minute drive
