Booking Policy

  • MAKING A BOOKING

    Bookings should preferrably be made through contacting us directly by sending an email to our bookings email bookings@idwalalam.com. Bookings made through other channels often include commission which means additional charges on your part.

  • PAYMENT FOR BOOKINGS

    Payments for bookings can be made either through internet banking or credit card. Guests are required to pay 50% for off-peak and 100% of the invoice for peak season bookings.

Cancellation Policy

  • CANCELLING A BOOKING - PEAK SEASON

    Peak season bookings refer to any bookings made during December/January, special long and sports weekends. A full refund will be paid to customers for these bookings provided we receive notification of cancellation thirty days before the booking. If a cancellation is not made thirty days before but still falls outside 14 days before the actual appointment, the customer forfeits the deposits but will not be liable for the full amount due.Cancellations for peak season bookings will not be accepted if the date of the booking is less than 14 days from cancellation date, customers will be liable for full amount of booking in this case.

  • CANCELLING A BOOKING - OFF PEAK

    Off peak season bookings refer to any bookings made outside peak season. A full refund will be paid to customers for these bookings provided we receive notification of cancellation seven days before the booking. If a cancellation is not made seven days before but still falls outside 48 hours before the actual appointment, the customer forfeits the deposits but will not be liable for the full amount due.Cancellations for off peak bookings will not be accepted if the date of the booking is less than 14 days from cancellation date, customers will be liable for full amount of booking in this case.

Terms & Conditions

  • EARLY DEPARTURES

    Should a guest decide to leave before the booked departure date, full payment of the quoted room rate will be attracted for the originally booked dates up to a maximum of three days in addition to the number of days the guest has already stayed.We advise guests to ensure that travel insurance will cover any losses due to cancellation.
